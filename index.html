FAQ

Frequently Asked
Questions

What types of properties do you offer?

We offer a wide range of residential properties including approved residential plots (30×40 to 60×80), gated community sites, and luxury villa developments across prime locations in Bangalore. All properties come with clear titles, BDA/BMRDA approvals, and full legal documentation.

Where are your projects located?

Our projects are strategically located across Bangalore's high-growth corridors — Devanahalli (near the international airport), Sarjapur Road, Kanakapura Road, Whitefield, Electronic City, and Yelahanka. Each location is chosen for connectivity, appreciation potential, and quality of life.

How can I book a property?

Booking is simple! Contact us via phone, WhatsApp, or our website form. Our relationship manager will schedule a site visit, walk you through all documents, and guide you through the entire booking process — from plot selection to registration. We also offer home loan assistance through partner banks.

Do you provide legal assistance?

Absolutely. We have a dedicated legal team that ensures all transactions are fully transparent. We assist with title verification, sale agreement drafting, encumbrance certificate, and registration at the sub-registrar office. All our projects are RERA registered for your complete peace of mind.

Do you offer home loan assistance?

Yes! We have tie-ups with leading banks including SBI, HDFC, ICICI, and Axis Bank for easy home loan processing at competitive interest rates. Our finance team helps you with the entire loan application and disbursement process.
